Meet the Principal Architects

At the heart of ASHRAYA DESIGNS GROUP lies a partnership built on creativity, expertise, and a shared vision for transforming spaces. Together, Ar. Satish H.R and Ar. Usha Dongre has shaped the firm into a leading name in architecture and interior design, delivering projects that seamlessly blend innovation, functionality, and timeless beauty.

Ar. Satish H.R – Architect of Visionary Spaces

“Design isn’t just about structures; it’s about how people experience them.”
— Ar. Satish H.R

With a distinguished career spanning over 35 years, Ar. Satish H.R is celebrated for his creative vision and precise architectural execution. His philosophy goes beyond designing structures; he focuses on enhancing lives by crafting thoughtful, sustainable, and inspiring environments.

Ar. Usha Dongre – Architect of Elegance and Detail

“Elegance lies in the details — in every texture, every line, and every light that shapes the space.”
— Ar. Usha Dongre

With over 30 years of expertise in architecture and interior design, Ar. Usha Dongre is known for her refined sense of elegance, attention to detail, and deep commitment to creating spaces that are both beautiful and purposeful.
